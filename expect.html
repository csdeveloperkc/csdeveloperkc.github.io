For Your
Kids

We love families! Our "Faith Kids" program is a safe, fun, and high-energy environment for children from birth through 5th grade. Our background-checked volunteers are excited to help your kids learn about God's love.

Check-In Process

First-time family? Visit our Kids Check-In station in the lobby. We'll get your kids registered and show you to their age-appropriate classrooms securely.

Knowledge Base

Common Questions

What should I wear?

Come as you are! You'll see everything from jeans and t-shirts to business casual. We're more interested in meeting you than in what you're wearing.

Where are you located?

We are located at 249 E. Randall Ave, Rialto, CA. We have plenty of directional signs to help you find your way once you're on campus.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself! If you have a Bible, feel free to bring it, but we also project all the scriptures on the screen during the message.

waving_hand

Still have questions?

We'd love to help you feel completely comfortable for your first visit. Reach out to us anytime.
