The Worship Experience

What kind of music will I hear?
Our musical style would be described as traditional and contemporary. The song service can include a mixture of hymns, psalms, choirs, soloists, praise dancing, and choruses accompanied by a piano, drums, and guitar.

Common Questions

What should I wear?

While you will notice many of the people of Greater Faith Grace Bible Church and ministry leaders are wearing more tradition “Sunday” dress, there is no dress code to attend any of the weekly services.

Where are you located?

We are located at 249 E. Randall Ave, Rialto, CA. We have plenty of directional signs to help you find your way once you're on campus.

Will I hear a Bible Message?

The main focus for all of our services is point people to Jesus Christ. The preaching and teaching is to develop believers, connect non-believers to Jesus Christ, and bring those who have lost their way back to Jesus Christ.
