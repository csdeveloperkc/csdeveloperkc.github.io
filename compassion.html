Community Service

"Empower Unhoused lives so they can be independent, self-sufficient, healthier, physically, mentally and spiritually so they can care for themselves, eventually loved ones and become proud self sufficient community members."

inventory_2 Blessing Backpack

We provide access to information for short term housing, Health Care, Mental Health Care, Drug Rehabilitation, Technical Training, Veteran Rights, and Spiritual Support that can assist an individual become independent, self-sufficient and productive individuals in society.

Further support information is in our 'Blessing Backpack' – a physical resource guide and supply kit for those in immediate transition.
